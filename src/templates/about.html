About Us

Our Vision

Within our web-based platform, we've embraced the challenge of creating an AI-based tool for preliminary diagnosis of breast cancer. This undertaking is significant in a diverse nation like India, where access to specialized healthcare can be limited. Early detection of breast cancer is crucial, and we're building a seamless web application to make it accessible, allowing users to upload images for AI-driven preliminary diagnoses, essentially offering a 24/7 personal breast cancer screening tool within a web browser. Our vision is to revolutionize breast cancer detection and care through accessible, cutting-edge technology. Furthermore, we empower individuals with an AI-driven WhatsApp chatbot, providing education, preliminary risk assessments, personalized guidance, and seamless connection to healthcare professionals through integrated appointment booking. Our commitment is to improve survival rates, reduce healthcare burdens, and foster a holistic approach to breast cancer management by bridging the gap between advanced technology and widespread accessibility.
